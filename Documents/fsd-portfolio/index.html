Bio

I am a creative by nature with a focus on engaging with projects of creation. I've worked as a graphic designer for two years, desiging logos, concept art, graphics, and more. I'm an oil painter and a makeup artist, art has always been a large part of my life. Activism is a large part of my life, lifiting up those whom I can is a truly rewarding expirence. I've have been involved in demonstrations all over the midwest, supporting POC, LGBTQIA+, reproductive rights, and confronting all manner of oppression and marginalization. I love to crochet, anything from blankets, scarves, hats, and even spider webs, the latter being my current obession.
